Guy This Facebook Campaign Performance Dashboard showcases how August campaigns performed across weekdays, age groups, and genders, helping marketing teams quickly see what is working and where to optimize. It brings together key KPIs, spend patterns, and conversion insights in one clean, dark-themed view that is ready for stakeholder reporting.

Key metrics : 
Highlights total clicks, total spend, total conversions, and total impressions at the top so performance can be understood at a glance.
These high-level KPIs are supported by detailed tables and visuals for weekday, age, and gender, making it easy to move from overview to granular analysis.

Weekday performance : 
Multiple charts track impressions, spend, and approved conversions by weekday, helping identify the best-performing days for both visibility and ROI.
A cumulative spend visual over August shows how budget is utilized over time, useful for pacing and budget control discussions.

Audience insights : 
Age and gender visuals reveal which segments drive the most conversions, allowing more targeted audience strategies in future campaigns.
Supporting tables provide detailed breakdowns of impressions, clicks, spend, and conversions by age-gender combinations for deeper drill-down.

Channel efficiency : 
Trend visuals connect impressions, clicks, spend, and conversions to highlight how efficiently the campaign is converting reach into actions.
Daily impressions and click-through trends help spot peaks, drops, and potential opportunities for optimization in creative, timing, or targeting.
